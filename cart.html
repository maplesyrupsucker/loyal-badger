Woocommerce Loyalty Checkout

Exploring how to spend Simple tokens and Bitcoin Cash in a transactional scenario.

Step 1
Send Loyalty Token

We're using $HONK as a loyalty token. Sending tokens will update your cart with a discount on your order.

Step 2.
Proceed To Checkout

Loyal customers are treated to a discount which is passed along to checkout. Normal users pay full price. SLP callback could revise checkout with coupon code via:
?wc-ajax=apply_coupon

Step 3
Pay in BCH

Complete the payment in Bitcoin Cash.
